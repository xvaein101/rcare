    Refenste Day Care Centre
    Putting young children first

Refenste Day Care Centre
is a organisation, committed to
putting young children first by ensuring quality
early childhood development for our country’s
youngest citizens.

What we do

We provide training, resources and support in the field of early childhood development, enhancing individual and organisation capacity; we develop and disseminate resources; and we carry out research specifically related to the African context.

Where we work

We are based in a suburban township located in the middle of Kaalfontein and Allandale. we provide services and programmes country wide, increasing the effectiveness and efficiency of early childhood development provision to all South Africa’s children.

Sustaining our work

We sustain our work through self-generated income from project management, consultancy work, publications and research. Specific early childhood development programmes and projects are supported through grants from Foundations and Trusts, Government Departments and from Corporate Social Investment Programmes.

  • Quality early childhood development has profound
    effects on the development of young children, and life
    changing benefits for our communities and our country.
